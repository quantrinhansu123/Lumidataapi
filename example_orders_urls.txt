# Orders API - Các URL ví dụ sẵn sàng sử dụng

## Base URL
http://127.0.0.1:8000/orders

## 1. Lọc theo Team (Checkbox - Nhiều giá trị)
http://127.0.0.1:8000/orders?team=Morning,Afternoon,Night

## 2. Lọc theo Delivery Status (Checkbox)
http://127.0.0.1:8000/orders?delivery_status=Delivered,Pending,In Transit

## 3. Lọc theo Payment Status (Checkbox)
http://127.0.0.1:8000/orders?payment_status=Paid,Unpaid,Pending

## 4. Lọc theo Delivery Staff (Checkbox)
http://127.0.0.1:8000/orders?delivery_staff=Nguyễn Văn C,Trần Văn D,Lê Văn E

## 5. Lọc theo Marketing Staff (Checkbox)
http://127.0.0.1:8000/orders?marketing_staff=Nguyễn Đức Anh,Trần Văn Bình

## 6. Lọc theo Country (Checkbox)
http://127.0.0.1:8000/orders?country=VN,US,UK

## 7. Lọc theo Product (Checkbox)
http://127.0.0.1:8000/orders?product=Product A,Product B,Product C

## 8. Lọc theo City (Checkbox)
http://127.0.0.1:8000/orders?city=Hà Nội,Hồ Chí Minh,Đà Nẵng

## 9. Lọc theo Date Range
http://127.0.0.1:8000/orders?from_date=01/02/2026&to_date=10/02/2026&date_column=order_date

## 10. Kết hợp Team + Delivery Status
http://127.0.0.1:8000/orders?team=Morning,Afternoon&delivery_status=Delivered

## 11. Kết hợp Team + Payment Status
http://127.0.0.1:8000/orders?team=Morning,Afternoon&payment_status=Paid

## 12. Kết hợp Delivery Staff + Delivery Status
http://127.0.0.1:8000/orders?delivery_staff=Nguyễn Văn C,Trần Văn D&delivery_status=Delivered

## 13. Kết hợp Team + Delivery Status + Payment Status
http://127.0.0.1:8000/orders?team=Morning,Afternoon&delivery_status=Delivered,Pending&payment_status=Paid,Unpaid

## 14. Kết hợp Marketing Staff + Product + Date Range
http://127.0.0.1:8000/orders?marketing_staff=Nguyễn Đức Anh,Trần Văn Bình&product=Product A,Product B&from_date=01/02/2026&to_date=10/02/2026

## 15. Kết hợp Delivery Staff + Team + Delivery Status + Date Range
http://127.0.0.1:8000/orders?delivery_staff=Nguyễn Văn C,Trần Văn D&team=Morning,Afternoon&delivery_status=Delivered&from_date=01/01/2026&to_date=31/01/2026

## 16. Kết hợp đầy đủ các filter quan trọng
http://127.0.0.1:8000/orders?team=Morning,Afternoon&delivery_status=Delivered,Pending&payment_status=Paid,Unpaid&delivery_staff=Staff1,Staff2&country=VN,US&product=Product A,Product B&from_date=01/01/2026&to_date=31/01/2026

## 17. Lọc theo Check Result
http://127.0.0.1:8000/orders?check_result=Pass,Fail

## 18. Lọc theo Sale Staff
http://127.0.0.1:8000/orders?sale_staff=Nguyễn Văn A,Trần Thị B

## 19. Lọc theo CSKH
http://127.0.0.1:8000/orders?cskh=Nguyễn Văn F

## 20. Lọc với Limit
http://127.0.0.1:8000/orders?team=Morning&delivery_status=Delivered&limit=50

## 21. Lọc theo Tracking Code
http://127.0.0.1:8000/orders?tracking_code=TRACK001,TRACK002

## 22. Lọc theo Customer Type
http://127.0.0.1:8000/orders?customer_type=VIP,Regular

## 23. Lọc theo Payment Method
http://127.0.0.1:8000/orders?payment_method=Bank Transfer,Cash

## 24. Lọc theo Shipping Unit
http://127.0.0.1:8000/orders?shipping_unit=Vietnam Post,FedEx

## 25. Lọc theo Created At (Date Range)
http://127.0.0.1:8000/orders?from_date=01/02/2026&to_date=10/02/2026&date_column=created_at
